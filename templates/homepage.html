Mindset

Mindset Journal is a free tool for you to use with your therapist to share what is on your mind. By recognising your mood, your gratitudes and your thoughts for the day, you can build a simple practice of self-care.
Daily entries will promote mindful thinking. Alongside CBT and mindfulness, gratitude and general journals are an effective method to condition your thinking and awareness for more constructive thoughts and reactions for better well-being.
